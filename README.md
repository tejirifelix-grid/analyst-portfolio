## Featured Project: PAYGo Solar Customer Repayment Analysis

Analyzed ~28,000 PAYGo solar contracts to identify repayment drivers.

**Key Insights**
* Past average payment per period strongly predicts future repayment (correlation 0.68)
* High segment repays ~9.3× more than Low segment on average
* Recommendation: Prioritize customers with avg historical payment > ~$1,200

**Tools**: Google Sheets, Power BI
