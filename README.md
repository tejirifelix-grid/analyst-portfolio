### 1. E-commerce Customer Retention & Repeat Purchase Optimization (New)
**Live Interactive Dashboard**: [View Power BI Report](https://app.powerbi.com/MobileRedirect.html?action=OpenReport&reportPage=6a26729d1b7002d88dbb&groupObjectId=32b8e66a-2440-4045-a43a-341053c2cec6&ctid=435e743f-161a-4029-b565-e4540ad693e8&reportObjectId=2abdb0a0-1013-4e2b-ae64-cb6737052f24&Context=share-report&bookmarkGuid=bef8819d-a048-4663-b2bf-b48117776b59&pbi_source=mobile_ios)

**Static PDF Version** (for non-Power BI users): [Download E-commerce Retention PDF](./Ecommerce-Customer-Retention-Analysis/Ecommerce_Customer_Retention_Analysis_Tejiri.pdf)

**Project Folder**: [Ecommerce-Customer-Retention-Analysis](./Ecommerce-Customer-Retention-Analysis)

**Key Insights**
- Analyzed 805,549 cleaned transactions and 5,878 unique customers from the UK Online Retail II dataset
- **72.4%** of customers (4,255) are repeat buyers, driving **96.8%** of **£17.74 million** total revenue
- One-time buyers (27.6%) contribute only **3.2%** of revenue
- Cohort retention starts strong (Dec 2009: 91.2%) but declines gradually, with highest risk in the **first 1–3 months**

**Recommendations**
- Launch targeted re-engagement campaigns in the critical 30–90 day window for one-time buyers
- Implement loyalty programs to protect the high-value repeat segment (96.8% of revenue)
- Use simple customer segmentation to prioritize retention efforts

**Tools**: Power BI (Power Query + DAX), Cohort Analysis

### 2. PAYGo Solar Customer Repayment Analysis
Analyzed ~28,000 PAYGo solar contracts to identify repayment drivers.

**Key Insights**
- Past average payment per period strongly predicts future repayment (correlation 0.68)
- High segment repays ~9.3× more than Low segment on average
- Recommendation: Prioritize customers with avg historical payment > ~$1,200

**Tools**: Google Sheets, Power BI
