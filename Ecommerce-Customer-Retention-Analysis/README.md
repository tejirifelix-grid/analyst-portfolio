# E-commerce Customer Retention & Repeat Purchase Optimization

**Project Overview**  
End-to-end analysis focused on customer retention and repeat purchase optimization using the real UK Online Retail II dataset. The goal was to identify why customers churn after the first purchase and recommend actions to increase repeat revenue.

**Business Problem**  
Customers frequently make one purchase but do not return, resulting in high acquisition costs and lost lifetime value.

**Key Insights**
- Analyzed **805,549 cleaned transactions** and **5,878 unique customers**
- **72.4%** of customers are repeat buyers, but they drive **96.8%** of **£17.74 million** total revenue
- One-time buyers (27.6%) contribute only **3.2%** of revenue
- Cohort retention starts strong (e.g. Dec 2009 cohort: 91.2%) but shows gradual decline, with highest risk in the first 1–3 months

- Implement loyalty tactics to protect the high-value repeat segment
